ReadSpeaker’s partnership with corporate learning platform aNewSpring enables Text to Speech to be integrated quickly and easily into its learning environment, facilitating accessibility. In this piece, we talk to aNewSpring’s Business Development Director Robbert Ladan about corporate upskilling, and why learning accessibility is becoming central to developing real workforce capability. Many organisations still treat accessibility as a compliance exercise. Training content is audited against standards, accommodations are made if and when requested, and the requirement is considered fulfilled.
